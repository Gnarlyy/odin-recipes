Minestrone Soup

"This minestrone soup recipe features vegetables, beans, and pasta in a rich, tomato-based broth."

  • Margarine
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped carrots
  • Chopped celery
  • Chicken broth
  • Cannellini beans
  • Stewed tomatoes
  • Cubed potatoes
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Dried parsley
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Elbow macaroni
  • Parnesan cheese
  1. Melt margarine in a heavy stockpot over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery; saut√© until beginning to soften, 3 to 4 minutes. Add broth, beans, tomatoes, potatoes, cabbage, parsley, tomato paste, garlic, and salt; bring to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Laddle and garnish with Parmesan cheese
